It is a multi-system breakdown leading to a host of conditions, characterized by what we call a spleen and liver disharmony pattern, which I believe is the central root for gout(and many other health issues). In this book the central herbal formula is something called bu zhong yi qi tang, which is the base formula for addressing this yin fire.
